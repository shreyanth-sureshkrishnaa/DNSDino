Help

What is this website used for?

Welcome to DNSDino!

DNSDino is a platform designed to provide users with detailed DNS and WHOIS information for any domain, while also serving as an educational resource on the foundational technologies that power the internet!

DNSDino transforms domain analysis into a learning experience, making internet infrastructure more transparent and approachable.

What can you do on DNSDino?

  • Look up DNS records such as A, AAAA, MX, CNAME, and NS for any domain
  • Perform WHOIS lookups to find domain registration and ownership details
  • Understand how DNS and WHOIS work through simplified explanations
  • Use it as a learning companion for cybersecurity, networking, and OSINT basics

How do I use it?

To get started, simply enter a domain name into the search bar at the top of the page. DNSDino will fetch and display all available DNS and WHOIS data for that domain. DNSDino is designed for students, cybersecurity enthusiasts, network administrators, ethical hackers, and anyone who wants to demystify how the web works beneath the surface.
